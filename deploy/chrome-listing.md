# Chrome Web Store Listing

## Name

JobRoom Helper

## Summary

Copy LinkedIn job details into Job-Room without submitting the form.

## Detailed description

JobRoom Helper is a small browser extension for people who track job applications in Job-Room.

Open a LinkedIn job page, click READ LinkedIn job, then open Job-Room and click PASTE into Job-Room. The extension fills the recognized fields and choices, but it does not submit the form.

It can copy:

- Job title
- Company
- LinkedIn job URL
- Job location with Swiss city mapping
- Applied-on date when LinkedIn exposes it
- Default Job-Room choices such as electronic application, full-time workload, no RAV assignment, and result still open

Supported city defaults:

- Geneva, Geneve, Genève, Genf: Geneva
- Zurich, Zürich: Zürich
- Lausanne: Lausanne
- Bern, Berne: Bern

The extension runs only when you click its popup buttons. It stores the last read job locally in your browser so it can paste it into Job-Room on the next page.

## Single purpose

The extension reads job details from LinkedIn job pages and locally pastes them into Job-Room form fields when the user clicks the popup buttons.
