# Mac App Store Listing

## Name

JobRoom Helper for Safari

## Subtitle

Copy LinkedIn jobs into Job-Room

## Promotional text

Read a LinkedIn job page and paste the job details into Job-Room without submitting the form.

## Description

JobRoom Helper for Safari helps you transfer job application details from LinkedIn to Job-Room.

Open a LinkedIn job page, click READ LinkedIn job, then open Job-Room and click PASTE into Job-Room. The extension fills recognized fields and choices, but leaves final review and submission to you.

It can copy the job title, company, LinkedIn URL, location, and applied-on date when available. It also supports common Swiss city mappings such as Geneva, Zürich, Lausanne, and Bern.

The extension stores only the last job you read, locally in Safari extension storage. It does not create an account, does not use analytics, and does not send job data to an external server.
